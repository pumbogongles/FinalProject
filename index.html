ikigai: The Reason for Being

Fulfilling the needs of the wider world is one of the key concepts of ikigai. Against the backdrop of climate change and sustainability issues, we believe that there is a pressing need to reduce waste in this world. Hence, at Ikigai, we try to contribute to this need in a small but significant way by using bags from sustainable materials to do all our shopping.
